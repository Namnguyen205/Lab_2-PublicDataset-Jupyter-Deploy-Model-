# Lab 2 - Public Dataset, Data Processing va Baseline Deployment

Thu muc nay luu noi dung Lab 2 trong chuoi bai thuc hanh Deploying AI and IoT applications. Lab tap trung vao viec su dung public dataset cho bai toan AIoT, quan sat du lieu cam bien theo thoi gian, tao output minh hoa va danh gia baseline model.

## Muc tieu

- Lam quen voi public dataset trong boi canh AIoT.
- Quan sat va phan tich chuoi du lieu cam bien theo thoi gian.
- Tao cac bieu do minh hoa tren Kaggle/Jupyter.
- Ghi lai ket qua baseline de lam co so so sanh voi cac lab tiep theo.

## Noi dung chinh

- File `Lab_2 PublicDataset Jupyter Deploy Model ChiTiet.docx` chua phan huong dan va ghi chep chi tiet cua Lab 2.
- Thu muc `outputs/screenshots/` chua cac anh ket qua trong qua trinh chay va danh gia model.
- Cac hinh anh ket qua gom bieu do chuoi thoi gian CO2, anomaly score va confusion matrix.

## Ket qua

- `kaggle_co2_time_series.png`: bieu do chuoi thoi gian du lieu CO2/cam bien.
- `kaggle_anomaly_score.png`: bieu do diem bat thuong/anomaly score.
- `kaggle_confusion_matrix.png`: ma tran nham lan dung de danh gia baseline model.
